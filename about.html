The story of AKU Rowing Club

Founded by AKU students who wanted to row. Backed by the Office of Student Experience. Growing every season.

Our mission

Built for medical students

Rowing teaches discipline, pacing, and teamwork — three things that make for both a great rower and a great doctor. AKU Rowing Club was founded in 2023 to bring this sport to the AKU community for the first time.

We train on Concept2 ergometers at the AKU Sports & Rehabilitation Center for indoor sessions, and take to the creek at Karachi Boat Club for our outdoor on-water programme.
